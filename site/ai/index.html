STI AI

STI AI is the intelligence layer that reasons over SecureTheCloud’s labs, frameworks, and architectures — producing explainable insights, not black boxes.

What STI AI Will Do

  • Correlate labs to STI Shield domains
  • Identify control coverage gaps
  • Recommend next labs or learning paths
  • Explain architectural risk posture

Design Constraints

  • Explainable reasoning
  • No black-box scoring
  • Architecture-aware
  • Human-in-the-loop

Status

STI AI is currently in the design and correlation phase. Implementation will follow validated architecture and data models.
