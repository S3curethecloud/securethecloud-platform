Identity Shield

Identity Shield establishes identity as the primary security control plane for modern cloud and hybrid environments.

Purpose

Identity Shield ensures that every access request is authenticated, authorized, and continuously verified — regardless of network location.

Core Principles

  • Centralized identity authority
  • Federated access
  • Least privilege by default
  • Non-human identity governance

Threats Addressed

  • Credential theft
  • Privilege escalation
  • Over-permissioned service accounts
  • Identity sprawl

Validated by Labs

Identity Shield patterns are validated through hands-on labs, including workforce federation, SCIM provisioning, and identity-centric access control.
